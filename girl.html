Themes to choose from

Outer Space Party
Guests will decorate their own rock using sharpies. They will then use foil paper to create the best astronaut gloves and moon walk while the play red light, green light. Lastly, our astronauts in training will play games like Grab a Planet, the Weightlessness Game and the Black Hole where teams try to throw objects into a black hole in the dark.
Royalty Creations
Guests will arrive dressed in their princess/prince costumes. Dress up outfits will be given to children who do not come dressed. Guests will then create their own magic wands or swords, have a story read to them, learn to act like royalty, followed by a princess/prince crowning ceremony.
Tea Party
Guests will share a time of elegance with this fancy tea party. Classical music will fill their air as guests are given “ladies hats” and white gloves to wear. They will then meet by the gazebo for a story and have a “book talk”. Lemonade will then be served along with crackers followed by a toast in honor of the birthday child.
Pajama Party
Guests will arrive dressed in their pajamas. They will use fabric markers to decorate their own eye masks. They will then eat snacks in our tent/gazebo where they will tell stories in the dark, get their hair braided and engage in slumber party games.
Rock Star Choreography
Female guests will start off by painting their nails and sporting fishnet rock star gloves. Boys will wear bandanas and design their own guitar pick necklaces. Guests will then work together to learn a dance, which they will perform on stage followed by a glow in the dark dance party.
Superhero Party
Guests will dress up in capes and masks and create their own superhero cuffs. They will then shoot down villains with bean bags, jump thru hoola hoops, use their x-ray vision to guess what’s in the bag, complete an adventurous obstacle course and try to get it all done with the best time.
A Mermaid Party
Guests will dress in mermaid tails, glitter their hair and paint their nails in shimmering colors. Boys will wear fish hats and sculpt under the sea sculptures using modeling clay. All guests will then paint an under the sea painting on a mini canvas and play mermaid sea games like Save the Sea creature and mermaid tail race.
An Art Party
Guests will dress in smocks and painter’s hats. They will then complete a beading, wooden craft or original painting on a mini canvas. Their creativity will then be put to the test as they are asked to work as a team to complete tasks like creating the longest chain out of one sheet of paper, using toilet paper to create the best mummy and creating the best robot using one roll of aluminum paper.
Carnival Games
Guests will be stamped with carnival themed tattoos and dressed in clown hats and noses as a sign of their admittance into the Festivities. They will then get their game on with a glow in the dark ring toss game followed by a spoon relay, back to back , a potato sack race and an all time favorite … pie face!
Beauty School Party
Guests will be given a robe and a spa headband for lounging. They will then follow step by step directions on how to apply face masks, nail polish, make up, and jeweled tattoos to one another.
Sports party
Guests will have their faces painted with war paint to show their game readiness. They will then have an active warmup to prepare them for the competitions ahead followed by games like hoover soccer, human bumper balls and spike ball.
Baby Shower
This custom party’s program will vary depending on the honoree’s request. Be ready for a night full of baby shower games and entertainment that will leave your guests wanting more.
Bridal Shower
Each bridal shower party will have a unique program that will include bridal shower games, team activities and more. There’s no better way to celebrate a soon to be bride than with Heavenly Joy entertainment.
Graduation Parties
The programs for graduation parties will be uniquely tailored for each party. However, games like Are you Smarter than a Fifth Grader and Minute to Win it are sure to put the graduate’s skills to the test in this fun filled night.
