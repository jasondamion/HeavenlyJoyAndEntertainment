Who are we?


Heavenly Joy is an event venue that is perfect for all of your small celebrations. We pride ourselves in our entertainment during all festivities and we are sure that we can make your celebration a heavenly experience.


Birthday Parties

All birthday celebrations begin with themed activities that take approximately 30 minutes. Guests are then brought out onto the dance floor where they engage in a glow in dark dance party. Children are then invited to our banquet style dining table where they share some words for the birthday child and then re-energize with some pizza and juice. The festivities continue with some high energy games which our clients will choose beforehand. The night culminates with a cake cutting ceremony and pictures that will last a lifetime.

Custom Parties

Custom Parties vary greatly in itinerary. However, since these three hour parties usually have a guest list of 60 guests, we like to start off the party with some finger foods and by inviting guests to take advantage of our digital photo booth. We then kick off the night with an ice breaker or two that will encourage guests to socialize with one another. Afterwards, the guest of honor usually delivers a speech and guests enjoy their dinner while a slideshow is projected on our screen. After dinner, its all fun and games with our competitive tournaments that will fill the venue with the energy every heavenly celebration needs.
